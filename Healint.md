about Healint: 

We are here to improve the lives of the one billion people suffering from painful conditions**

Our goal is to help patient to find the right treatment at the right time. One of the ways to achieve it, is to speed up the market reach of new treatments.

We bring Scale, Granularity and Reliability to clinical trials.

It all began with a medical emergency

In 2013, Francois Cadiou, co-founder of Healint, had no inkling that his father had just suffered a stroke. In fact, no one did - except for the people who were treating an unknown man at a French hospital.

Francois set out to develop an app that would allow people experiencing a major health crisis to get help (and notify their family members) by simply shaking their phone. One thing led to another, and soon he realised there were numerous uses for the technology he and his team had developed. Through observing behavior, stress, and other factors, it was possible not only to predict strokes but also a migraine attack.

In 2014, Francois and the team launched Migraine Buddy; today, it is the #1 migraine patient app in the world with over 3,000,000 people using it globally to manage their condition. Based on our knowledge of the disease and the conduction of clinical trials, we developed tools that allow to dramatically speed up trials and ensure a faster market reach. Our capabilities now expand far beyond migraine and we manage studies in a large range of conditions related to the **Central Nervous System** or the **Immune System**.

> Our objective is for Healint's entire suite of services to revolutionize the way studies are conducted.
