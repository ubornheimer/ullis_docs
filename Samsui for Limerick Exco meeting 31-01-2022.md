5.  vote for the different leadership positions one at a time

	-   Spirit Captain

Comment on step 2: Since our team members are quite mature, don’t seem to have a lot of hierarchical tendencies and are generally quite busy with work and family, I think it is better to have people with the mental and time capacity step up by their own initiative for those captain positions (plan 2A). Only if we don’t get enough candidates, we can ask/urge people to take on the responsibility (plan 2B).

on another note: from experience with the beach training, we should be more stringent with the groups of 5 during training. After all, we have a number of expats on the team who care about their work visa and we should all care about how Ultimate is perceived in the SG public. I'd suggest the following measures:
	- keep on assigning clear groups of 5 before the training
	- assign clear spatially separated meeting points for those groups before the training to prevent mingling before and after training and during water breaks  
  
I can offer my policing expertise, but I don’t enjoy being the party pooper. If no one else steps up to help with that, I might get quite frustrated…
