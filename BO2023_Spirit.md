# In-Game Manners

Discuss the following points

## 1) NEVER, EVER, HIT AN OPPONENT WITH THE DISC!!!!!!!!!
- not for "checking in" the disc, nor in any other situation - VERY RUDE!
- the DEFENSE player is responsible for checking in the disc. So, as the offense player with the disc, hold it in front of you and offer it to the defense player to tap it in.
- more on checking in the discs a bit later
