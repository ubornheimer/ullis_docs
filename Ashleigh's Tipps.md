# posting
- feet back by 10cm (shoulder-hips-heels vertical)
- stand up from front padding of saddle
- seat is hot: stand up fast
- drive horse forward with purpose
- close eyes to feel twisting of wrong hand in posting

# general posture
- alternate: tilt hips backwards, lift chest (whips under axel picture)
- push shopping cart forward for positive tension
- brake shopping cart for slowing down
- lower knees instead of heels

# driving
- two short kicks in quick succession
